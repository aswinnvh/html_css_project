Revolt RV 400

The Revolt RV400 is powered by 3000 watt Mid Drive Motor. The Revolt RV400 takes 4.5 Hours to get fully charged its 3.24 KWh battery and has a claimed range of 80 km/charge. The Price of Revolt RV400 starts at Rs 1.25 Lakh. It is available in one variants, STD. It comes with Disc brake in both front and rear wheels. Revolt RV 400 has got some additional features like Ambient Light Sensor, Battery Status, Parking Signal, Locate my motorcycle.
