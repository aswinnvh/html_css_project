HOP Electric OXO

The Hop Oxo is powered by 6300 watt BLDC Hub motor Motor. The Hop Oxo takes 5 Hours to get fully charged its 3.75 Kwh battery and has a claimed range of 70 km/charge. The Price of Hop Oxo starts at Rs 1.25 Lakh and goes up to Rs 1.40 Lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). It is available in two variants, STD and X. Hop Oxo is available in 4 colours.
